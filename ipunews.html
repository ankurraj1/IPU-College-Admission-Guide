2. COUNSELLING

IPU CET Counselling 2017 - Earlier, the last date for registration of IPU CET counselling 2017 was June 16. The last date for online fee payment, registration, verification of reserved category certificates have also been extended till June 19, June 20 and June 22 respectively. The result for IPU CET 2017 was announced on June 2 for all B.Tech programmes, along with the result for B.Tech Bio-Technology. IPU CET 2017 counselling will be held online which includes the process of online registration, choice filling, document verification and seat allotment for the candidates who have qualified in IPU CET 2017. Candidates have to register for IPU CET counselling from June 13, 2017 after paying the counselling fee online or offline. The counselling of IPU CET 2017 is being conducted in 3 rounds with open house and spot round. Candidates are required to attend the counselling of IPU CET 2017 on the basis of their ranks in IPU CET. Candidates need to fill the choices and submit their documents for verification before seat allotment.

3. IMPORTANT DATES

IPU CET Important Dates 2017 have been announced by Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), for candidates who are seeking admissions to undergraduate engineering programmes across participating institutes affiliated to the university. IPU CET 2017 was conducted on May 20 (2.30 pm to 5 pm). The last date to fill the application form of IPU CET 2017 was May 1, 2017. The official result of the entrance examination has been announced on June 2, 2017. The IPU CET 2017 important dates list detailed schedule regarding entrance exam like opening & closing dates of registration, application form availability, admit card, result, counselling and seat allotment. The important dates of IPU CET 2017 help aspiring candidates to have clarity over every event associated with the exam in advance. It also prevents the IPU CET candidates from any last minute trouble.
