About IPU CET 2017

IPU CET 2017- Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has started the IPU CET counselling 2017 from June 13, 2017 onwards. Earlier the counselling of IPU CET 2017 was supposed to begin from June 9, 2017. GGSIPU conducted the IPU CET 2017 on May 20, 2017 (2:30 pm to 5 pm) for all B.Tech programmes (except B.Tech Bio-Tech) offering admissions to various undergraduate engineering courses. The IPU CET admit card 2017 was available for downloading from May 11 onwards for registered candidates who have to login and download the same. Application form for B.Tech Bio-Technology was available till May 1, while the exam for this programme conducted on May 29 (2 pm to 5 pm). IPU CET 2017 for B.Tech Biotechnology has been postponed by 8 days. Earlier the exam was scheduled to be held on May 21. Aspirants, who fulfill the eligibility criteria, could apply online for IPU CET 2017 from February 16 onwards till May 1. Application and application fee payment both could be done online. The university has declared the result of IPU CET 2017 on June 2. On the basis of merit/ rank obtained by the test takers in IPU CET 2017, qualifying candidates will be offered admissions to 5,260 B.Tech seats across 81 affiliated private institutes and 690 B.Tech seats in 35 affiliated government institutes through IPU CET.
