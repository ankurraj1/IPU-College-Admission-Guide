GGSIPU B.Tech Counselling

IPU CET Counselling 2017 - The counselling of IPU CET 2017 has started with online registration and paying the participation fee from June 13. The participation fee of IPU CET 2017 can be paid online or offline. IPU CET 2017 counselling is being conducted by Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) for admissions to undergraduate engineering programmes at the varsity and the engineering institutes affiliated to it. GGSIPU has extended the registration deadline from June 16 to June 19 now. Earlier, the last date for registration of IPU CET counselling 2017 was June 16. The last date for online fee payment, registration, verification of reserved category certificates have also been extended till June 21, June 20 and June 22 respectively. The result for IPU CET 2017 was announced on June 2 for all B.Tech programmes, along with the result for B.Tech Bio-Technology. IPU CET 2017 counselling will be held online which includes the process of online registration, choice filling, document verification and seat allotment for the candidates who have qualified in IPU CET 2017. Candidates have to register for IPU CET counselling from June 13, 2017 after paying the counselling fee online or offline. The counselling of IPU CET 2017 is being conducted in 3 rounds with open house and spot round. Candidates are required to attend the counselling of IPU CET 2017 on the basis of their ranks in IPU CET. Candidates need to fill the choices and submit their documents for verification before seat allotment. Read on to check all details related to IPU CET Counselling 2017 below in this article.

IPU CET Counselling 2017 - (First Counselling)

Qualifying candidates will be called for counselling and their list will be published one week (5 working days) prior to commencement of counselling.The counselling for B.Tech Biotechnology will be conducted separately (from all other B.Tech programmes) in offline mode. They must personally report to the notified notified venue as per the date and time for counselling/admission. They must carry their documents mentioned in the counselling schedule. He/she must mark the attendance which is with the admission officer. Then Seat Allotment to these candidates will be done while they attend the counselling personally. Candidates now need to pay the counselling fee of Rs. 1000 (non-refundable). Further they have to produce their required documents for verification. After seat allotment, the candidate is required to pay the notified fee immediately on the spot at the time of counselling/admission. They must check all the details on the admission slip they will be given by the admission officer.

IPU CET 2017 Counselling - (Second Counselling )

The second counselling will be held for vacant seats in all categories. Candidates who got a seat in first counselling and paid the counselling fee need not to pay the counselling fee in second counselling. Those who didn't pay the counselling fee have to pay the Rs. 1,000 as counseling fee in second counselling. Candidates of first counselling can appear for second counselling for upgradation/change the allotted programme and institute. But for that they have to pay an additional sum of Rs. 5,000 paid via Demand Draft in favour of Registrar, Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, payable at Delhi.

IPU CET 2017 Counselling Round III:

There will be a third rould of counselling too on the lines of round I and II. Students should not loose hope if they have not got any college in First or Second Counselling as in third counselling Cut-Offs get dropped by significant margin which may ensure students with slightly lesser ranks to secure a college seat.

Open House/Spot Round of Counselling:

This round will be conducted if seats remain vacant. This round is the most awaited round of counselling as students falling under Outside-Delhi catagory are likely to get colleges in this round of catagory. It is to be noted that only Unoccupied seats of colleges get filled up in this counsiling and this counseling is not centralised i.e. students need to be physically present in the college premises with all required doccumentation and the Demand Draft.  IMP  Information regarding spot councelling is provided by the respective college website and not by GGSIPU website.
