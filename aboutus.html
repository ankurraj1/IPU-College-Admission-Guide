IPU College Admission Guide is a website to help students getting information about Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, i.e. one of the top most ranked Universities of India. IPU College Admission Guide helps students to get the proper guidance for getting admitted in a B.Tech program in Colleges affiliated to G.G.S.I.P.U.

What do we provide?

  • List of colleges affiliated to G.G.S.I.P.U.
  • Information about the courses offerred.
  • College predictor.
  • Guidance and counselling.
